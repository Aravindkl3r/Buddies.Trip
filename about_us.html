About Buddies@Trip.com

During our training period at TCS, we were each placed in different rooms, but somehow our paths crossed during breaks and activities, and we quickly formed connections as we embarked on our journey to kickstart our careers. Though we came from diverse backgrounds, we quickly discovered our shared passion for traveling during our lunch breaks and downtime discussions. This common interest sparked a bond that grew stronger each day

One morning, after an intense week of training, one of us suggested taking a trip to Kadamakudi to watch the sunrise. We all eagerly agreed, craving a break from our routines and looking forward to a new adventure. We made plans to meet early the next morning, packing our cameras to capture the breathtaking views

The next day, as we watched the sun rise over the serene backwaters of Kadamakudi, we were captivated by the beauty of the moment. The sky was painted in a brilliant palette of colors, and the tranquil environment provided a much-needed escape from our work lives. We laughed and shared stories as we took in the picturesque scene, knowing we had created a memory that would bond us even more From that point on, we decided to explore new places together whenever we could, forming a travel team we affectionately named Buddies@Trip.com.
